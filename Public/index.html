Core Framework

SENSE → ANALYZE → PRIORITIZE → RESPOND → EVALUATE

The framework transforms distributed city-level emergency data into actionable operational intelligence for command centers and response agencies.

01. Sense

Collect incident reports, SOS alerts, GPS locations, CCTV observations, weather data, and barangay reports.

02. Analyze

Classify incident type, location, severity, available responders, historical frequency, and risk patterns.

03. Prioritize

Generate risk levels such as Low, Moderate, High, and Critical to support rapid decision-making.

04. Respond

Recommend nearest responder, required agency, suggested action, and estimated urgency.

05. Evaluate

Measure response time, incident resolution, hotspots, responder performance, and decision accuracy.
